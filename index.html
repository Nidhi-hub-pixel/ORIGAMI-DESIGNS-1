Interesting Facts about Chameleon

  • Chameleons are reptiles that are part of iguana suborder
  • Changing skin color is an important part of communication among Chameleons
  • Most Chameleons have prehensile tail that they use to wrap around tree branches

Interesting Facts about camel

  • Camel's ears are furry and they are called as "THE SHIP OF DESERT"
  • Camel can easily move across the sand because they have specially designed feet
  • When they find water, they will drink it as much as possible and store it in their hump till they get water again

Interesting facts about pigeon

  1. Pigeons are incredibly complex and intelligent animals
  2. Pigeons are renowned for their outstanding navigational abilities
  3. Pigeons have excellent hearing abilities
